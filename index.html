The Temptations
The Temptations are a Detroit, Michigan-based vocal group that had a string of hit singles and albums with Motown Records in the 1960s and 1970s. The members of the band are known for their choreography, unique harmonies, and fashion sense. The Temptations are one of the most influential groups in mainstream music, having sold tens of millions of records.
Members
